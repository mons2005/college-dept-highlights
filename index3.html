Our College was established in the year 1998. It is promoted and supported by Virudhunagar Hindu Nadars' Devasthanam, various Hindu Nadars' Mahamai Tharappus in Virudhunagar and other places and educational institutions of Virudhunagar. Our College is ideally located on the home land of Karmaveerar "Bharat Ratna" K. Kamaraj and our institution is one of the Virudhunagar's most recognisable landmark. At KCET, our mission is to empower students with knowledge and skills that not only meet global standards but also cater to the evolving needs of industries. We strive to cultivate a culture of innovation, research, and ethical practices among our students, preparing them to become leaders in their chosen fields.Our academic programs are designed to blend theoretical knowledge with practical applications, ensuring our graduates are well-prepared to tackle real-world challenges. We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in engineering and technology, supported by a dedicated faculty known for their expertise and commitment to student success.
